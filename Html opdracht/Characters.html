Characters

The Hero of Dragon Quest is a descendant of Erdrick who has set out to rid the kingdom of Alefgard of the evil tyranny of the dreaded Dragonlord.

Erdrick:A hero who rescued Alefgard years earlier. Left a message for his descendant, in the cave which bears his name.

Lorik:the King of Tantegel, and ruler of Alefgard.

Princess Gwaelin:daughter of King Lorik. Imprisoned in the Swamp Cave south of Kol, by the servants of the Dragonlord.

The Dragonlord The villain of the story, he has stolen the Sphere of Light in order to lock Alefgard in perpetual darkness.
